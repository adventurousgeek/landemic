Landemic is a fun, blockchain-powered, property collectibles game that blurs the boundaries between the real and virtual world.

What is Landemic?

Landemic is a land rush Ethereum token where you buy tiles from a map of the Earth. Each tile covers approximately 275 x 275 m², which is about the size of Buckingham Palace.

What are Tiles?

When you buy a Landemic tile, you are buying a digital plot of land defined by an 8-character Google Plus Code, a new location standard created by Google and listed on every Google Maps location. Tiles are one-of-a-kind and 100% owned by you; they cannot be replicated, taken away, or destroyed.

What kind of tiles can I collect?

You can buy tiles that contain the white house, the Eiffel Tower, your favorite sports team arena, or your home!

What can do I with my tiles?

Anyone in the world can buy, sell and claim ownership interest in the land tile transactions, which bolsters the value of your land.

The Google Effect

Landemic is riding on the coattails of the best-kept secret in navigation: Google Plus Codes. Plus Codes are a new location standard that Google is promoting on every Maps search. The codes are meant to be a human-friendly substitute for latitude and longitude. They are 8-digit, alphanumeric codes, such as 87G8P2C2+, and they each represent a 275 x 275 m² patch of Earth. Consider them like IP addresses for location. Each tile on Landemic is represented by a unique Google Plus Code.
